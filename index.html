Vision Mission Objectives

Vision for the IDEA Lab

Working closely with businesses and entrepreneurs, we strive to stay at the forefront of the teaching-learning process, nurture creative thinking, and create innovation-driven products that address issues faced by rural populations in India as well as other complex and difficult problems that affect the real world.

Learn More

Mission for the IDEA Lab

Create an inspiring ambience to nurture innovative ideas, to implement project-based learning and to develop innovation driven products.Develop an ecosystem to work closely with Indian rural masses, Industries and Entrepreneurs.

Learn More

Objectives

The purpose of IDEA Lab is to provide all facilities under one roof, for conversion of an idea into a prototype.
With these facilities in the campus, more students and faculty will be encouraged to take up creative work.The focus will be on training students/faculty so that they become imaginative and creative and stay so at the workplaces they join.
